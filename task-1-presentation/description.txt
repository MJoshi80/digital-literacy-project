Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
CSE0001 | Megh S. Joshi 

Tool Used: Canva (canva.com)

What the Design Shows:
This infographic is a one-page visual resource designed to help first-year students
understand digital literacy and why it matters. It is divided into four sections:

  1. What is Digital Literacy?
     Explains what digital literacy means — the ability to use digital tools,
     communicate online, and navigate the internet safely and responsibly.

  2. Stay Safe Online
     Covers key safe internet practices including using strong passwords, enabling
     two-factor authentication, avoiding suspicious links, and logging out of
     shared devices.

  3. Build Your Digital Identity
     Highlights the importance of a professional online presence on platforms like
     GitHub and LinkedIn, and the idea that what you post online is a permanent
     digital footprint.

  4. Communicate Professionally
     Outlines the basics of email etiquette — clear subject lines, formal greetings,
     concise body text, and proper sign-offs.
